## Overview


**Bill of Materials (BOM) Description**



The Bill of Materials (BOM) lists all electronic components required for the 3D positioning system 
prototype. It includes part names, quantities, unit and total costs, manufacturers, manufacturer part 
numbers, supplier information, and schematic reference designators. The BOM ensures accurate component 
sourcing and cost estimation for both prototype and production builds, while each component also includes 
its designated supplier link and datasheet.

The Bill of Materials (BOM) lists all components required for the 3D positioning system prototype. The main 
components include:

- **Motion / Speed Sensors:** AS5600L-ASOM + AS5600-ASOM  
- **Voltage Regulators:** TLV73312PDBVR (3.3V) + TPS565242DRLR (5V)  
- **LED Indicators:** LTST-C190GKT  
- **Barrel Jack / Jumpers:** PJ-070AH-SMT-TR
- **Servo Motors (Control Fins):** 1142 RC Servo Motor  

These components, together with supporting resistors and capacitors, make possible the 3.3 V and 5 V power 
rails, and enable control of the fins from the ESP32-S3-WROOM-N4 microcontroller.
